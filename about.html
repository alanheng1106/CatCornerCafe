About Us

Welcome to Cat Corner Cafe — your cozy neighborhood cat café, where coffee meets purrs! We’re four friends brought together by a shared love of cats, community, and a really good cup of coffee.

Whether you’re here for the lattes or the lounging cats, we’re so glad you stopped by. Come in, grab a seat, and stay awhile — the cats are waiting!


How it all began?

Cat Corner Cafe was born from late-night chats, shared dreams, and way too many cat videos. The four of us always imagined a place where people could unwind with a warm drink and some feline company.

So, we combined our passions for coffee, community, and all things whiskered to create a café that feels like home (but with more cats). What started as an idea over coffee turned into a cozy little corner of joy — and we’re so excited to share it with you.
